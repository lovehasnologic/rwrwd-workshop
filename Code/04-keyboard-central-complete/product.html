Das Keyboard Model S Ultimate

Addiction Never Looked So Good

Type faster than you ever thought possible on one of these blank babies. Efficient typists don’t look down at the keys so why bother labeling them? Even better, it's addictive tactile feedback allows you to have a faster, more accurate typing experience. Be prepared for envious glares.

Tactile Feedback Overachievers Crave

Tactile and audio feedback so accurate it allows you to execute every keystroke with lightning-fast precision. Its high-performance, gold-plated mechanical key switches offer distinct tactile experiences adaptable to your personal taste - click and soft. Gold plated switches provide the best contact and typing experience because, unlike other metals, gold does not rust and lasts over time.

Free typing euphoria included.

Uber Quality

When Germans design it, you know it has to be good. We make products for overachievers who expect only the best. The Das Keyboard Ultimate Mechanical Keyboard is made of the highest-quality materials and robust construction you can feel.

Product Features
  • Mechanical Key Switches
  • USB 2.0 Hub
  • Extra Long USB Cable
  • N-key Rollover
Key Information
  • Cherry MX Blue mechanical key switches with gold contacts (clicky tactile)
  • No key cap inscriptions
  • Full n-key rollover with an included PS2 adapter, and 6 keys with USB
  • Enhanced 104-key layout
  • Bumps on F and J keys (home keys)
Technical Information
  • Glossy black upper enclosure with matching black USB cable
  • Two-port USB hub 1.1 and 2.0 compatible
  • Blue LEDs
  • Extra long two-meter (6.6-foot) USB cable
Even More Information
  • Dimensions: 18in x 6.5in x 1in (thickest portion, feet not extended, 1.5in w/ extended feet)
  • Weight: 1.36 kg (3 lbs)
  • Compatible with PS/2 and USB KVM switches.
System Requirements
  • PC or Mac, Windows, Linux, or Mac OS X
  • 2 USB ports or 1 USB port + 1 PS/2 port
  • USB to PS/2 adapter (included)
  • Media controls are not available using a PS2 connection
  • No driver required
Environmental Data
  • Operating temperature: 0°C to over 50°C
  • Operating humidity: 0% to 60%
Electrical Data
  • The USB hub has two ports and does not require an external power supply. It’s compatible with the 1.1 and 2.0 standards. Both ports can be active at the same time, but one can temporarily shut down if the current consumption is too high.
